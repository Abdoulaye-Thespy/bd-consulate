It is with profound honor and unwavering commitment that I assume the responsibilities of Honorary Consul-Designate of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh in Cameroon. This appointment represents more than a personal duty; it stands as a strong bridge between two friendly nations — a platform for shared opportunities and a pledge to work together for progress and prosperity. My vision is clear: To build an exemplary partnership between Cameroon and Bangladesh, founded on trust, respect, and mutual benefit. To expand economic and technological exchanges, harnessing Bangladesh’s industrial dynamism and Cameroon’s agricultural, energy, and cultural potential. To foster educational and scientific cooperation, empowering our young generations to shape a sustainable future. To strengthen solidarity and protection for the Bangladeshi community in Cameroon, ensuring their safety, integration, and active contribution to the local socio-economic fabric. We live in an era where global challenges — food security, energy transition, sustainable development — demand strategic alliances. By joining forces, Bangladesh and Cameroon can become a shining example of South–South cooperation. To the Cameroonian and Bangladeshi authorities, I extend my hand for constructive dialogue and concrete action. To the Bangladeshi nationals, I pledge to listen, support, and represent your interests with dignity. To the people of Cameroon, I invite you to discover the richness, resilience, and creativity of the Bangladeshi people, so that our exchanges are rooted in mutual knowledge and respect. Together, let us make this mandate not only a diplomatic honor, but a tangible driver of progress, fraternity, and shared opportunities.

NSANGOU MBOMBO ZOUNEDOU
